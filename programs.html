Program Overview

Zetech University offers two Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) programmes—in Computer Science and in Business Administration & Management—and three Master's programmes: Master of Science in Computer Science, Master of Science in Information Technology, and Master of Business Administration (MBA). These are available via full-time, part-time, and e-learning modes across multiple campuses, with intakes in January, May, and September.

Prospective students apply online through the University's SAJILI portal, submitting academic transcripts, proof of fee payment, and other supporting documents.

Doctoral Programs

PhD

Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science

Objective: To produce doctoral graduates who are problem solvers and innovative change agents in both academia and industry, capable of conducting advanced research in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, Cyber Security, and Mobile Computing.

Duration: Three (3) calendar years

Modes & Campuses: E-Learning, Full-Time, Part-Time at Mang'u Campus

Admission Requirements:

  • Master of Science in Computer Science or related field with upper second-class honors (or equivalent)
  • Master's degree with lower second-class honors plus ≥2 years' relevant work experience
  • Other qualifications as approved by Senate

PhD

Doctor of Philosophy in Business Administration & Management

Objective: To produce doctoral graduates who are innovative change agents in business management and administration, with expertise in areas such as Strategic Management, Finance, and Supply Chain Management.

Duration: Six (6) semesters (≈3 years)

Modes & Campuses: E-Learning, Full-Time, Part-Time at Mang'u, Nairobi City, and Thika Road (Ruiru) campuses

Admission Requirements:

  • Master's degree with first-class or upper second-class honors, or
  • Second-class lower division plus ≥2 years' work experience, or
  • Other qualifications approved by Senate

Master's Programs

MSc

Master of Science in Computer Science

Objective: To ground students in computational thinking, abstraction, logical reasoning, and implementation of solutions, with specializations in AI, Cyber Security & Computer Forensics, and Computer Networks.

Duration: Two (2) calendar years

Modes & Campus: E-Learning, Part-Time at Mang'u Campus

Admission Requirements:

  • Bachelor of Science in Computer Science or related field with upper second-class honors (or equivalent)
  • Second-class lower division plus ≥2 years' relevant experience, or
  • Other qualifications as approved by Senate

MSc

Master of Science in Information Technology

Objective: To produce IT professionals skilled in Software Engineering, Data Science, and Cyber Security & Computer Forensics, capable of leading large-scale projects and managing Big Data and ICT security.

Duration: Two (2) calendar years

Modes & Campuses: E-Learning, Part-Time at Mang'u, Thika Road, and Ruiru campuses

Admission Requirements:

  • Bachelor of Science in IT or related field with upper second-class honors (or equivalent)

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

Objective: To develop competent and innovative leaders who are change-makers in their areas of specialization in management.

Duration: Typically two (2) years

Modes & Campuses: E-Learning, Part-Time at Mang'u, Thika Road, and Ruiru campuses
